- is enabled by default with no additional cost
- SYN UDP Floods, Refelction attcks or other layer3 and layer4 attacks

- improves EC2, ELB, Cloudfront, Global Accel and Route 53

- 3k per month cost
- higher fees due to ddos are nulled
